About Me

Quality isn't a phase—
it's the whole process.

I'm a Software QA Engineer with 5+ years of experience helping Agile teams ship reliable, high-quality web and mobile applications — faster and with confidence.

My work spans the full QA spectrum: manual, API, and automation testing using Selenium and Playwright, integrated into CI/CD pipelines to catch defects before they reach users. I've led regression automation efforts that cut testing cycles by up to 90%, and delivered end-to-end coverage across complex, multi-platform products.

Beyond testing, I serve as a QA Lead and stakeholder bridge — collaborating directly with developers, product managers, and executive leadership to align quality standards with business goals. I don't just find bugs; I help build processes that prevent them.

Whether it's a loyalty platform, a biometric identification system, or a no-code workflow tool, I approach every project with the same commitment: reliable software that users can trust.
